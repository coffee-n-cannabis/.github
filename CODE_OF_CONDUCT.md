# Code of conduct

We're a small group of friends and colleagues running side projects. We don't have a roster, a code-of-conduct committee, or a manager to escalate to.

What we expect from anyone we work with, or who interacts with our work in public:

- Don't waste each other's time.
- Don't pressure people to be available outside agreed working hours.
- Don't require anyone to use their real name if pseudonymity works.
- Don't harass anyone here or elsewhere for who they are.
- Disagree about technical things — disagree as much as you like. Don't make it personal.

If somebody breaks these expectations, write to `info@coffee-n-cannabis.com`. We will read it and respond.

If we are the ones who broke them, tell us anyway.
